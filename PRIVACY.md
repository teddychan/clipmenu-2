# ClipMenu 2 — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-05_

ClipMenu 2 is a clipboard-history and snippet manager for macOS. It is built to keep
your data on your own devices.

## What ClipMenu stores
- **Clipboard history and snippets** you create are stored **locally on your Mac**.
- In the **Mac App Store edition**, while you have an active subscription, your snippets
  and settings sync through **your own private iCloud account** (Apple CloudKit).
  Clipboard history stays local on your Mac and is not synced to iCloud.

## What we do not collect
- ClipMenu does **not** send your clipboard contents, snippets, or any personal data to the
  developer or any third-party server.
- ClipMenu contains **no analytics, advertising, or tracking** SDKs.

## Permissions
- The **direct (non–App Store) edition** may request **Accessibility** permission solely to
  paste a selected clip into the frontmost app on your behalf. It is used only for that
  purpose. (The Mac App Store edition is sandboxed and does not use this.)

## Subscriptions and payments
- The **Mac App Store edition** is a paid app: a 30-day free trial, then a yearly
  auto-renewable subscription. Subscriptions are processed by **Apple** through the App
  Store — ClipMenu never sees or stores your payment information. You can manage or cancel
  anytime in **System Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions**.

## Data deletion
- Removing the app deletes its locally stored data. iCloud-synced data can be removed by
  clearing it in the app or via your iCloud storage settings.
